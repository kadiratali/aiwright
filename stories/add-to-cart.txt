Title: Add products to the shopping cart

As a logged-in customer
I want to add products to my shopping cart
So that I can purchase them later

Acceptance Criteria:
1. When the user adds a product to the cart, the cart badge in the header shows the total item count.
2. The "Add to cart" button of an added product turns into a "Remove" button.
3. The user can add multiple different products; the badge count increases accordingly.
4. When the user removes a product from the cart, the badge count decreases; if the cart becomes empty the badge disappears.

Technical notes (application under test: https://www.saucedemo.com):
- Login is required first (existing step: the user logs in as the "standard" user; existing fixture users: standard, locked_out).
- Product cards are .inventory_item, product name is .i
nventory_item_name.
- Add/remove buttons have data-test attributes like data-test="add-to-cart-sauce-labs-backpack" and data-test="remove-sauce-labs-backpack".
- The cart badge is .shopping_cart_badge inside the header.
